Toblerone is the best way to get transported into the Switzerland feeling, being the best swiss chocolate representation out there. It is produced in Bern, the city capital of Switzerland.

It is produced in the capital city of Switzerland, Bern. Its name comes from the inventor of Toblerone, Theodor Tobler, whose name is combined with the italian noun ’torrone’ which means nougat.

Toblerone was the first chocolate bar to have a filling. It comes in 10 different lengts and weights.
As one of the most iconic chocolate bars in the world, TOBLERONE has a rich history. Back in Switzerland, in 1908, Theodor Tobler and his cousin Emil Baumann invented the unique chocolate bar.
