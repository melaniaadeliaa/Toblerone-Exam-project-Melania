HOW IT'S MADE

Ingredients

It is produced in the capital city of Switzerland, Bern.

The ingredients needed for producing Toblerone chocolate are cacao beans, sugar and powdered milk. For the nougat: sugar, honey, egg white and almonds are also needed.

Chocolate making

The first step is roasting the cacao beans until it turns dark brown, so they can be peeled, crushed and grounded. When the mix turns into liquid chocolate, this mix becomes pure bitter cacao mass. After this, the milk, the sugar and the cacao mass are mixed roughly (those need to be stirred for a long time).

Nougat now

Nougat is made separately now. Honey is boiled until it forms bubbles. Then, icing sugar and water are mixed and boiled. Boiled honey and water-sugar mixture is combined now and boiled once more. Meanwhile, the separated egg whites are whipped and as the boiled mix cools down, everything is stirred together with almonds, and then cooled down.

Finishing up

After the nougat becomes cool and hard, it is chopped into small pieces then to be mixed with the chocolate.

The final chocolate is put into molds and cooled down, so it can be packaged and ready to be shipped to stores.
