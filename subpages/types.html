TYPES OF TOBLERONE

TOBLERONE milk chocolate

Smooth Milk chocolate with Honey and Almond Nougat. This delicious chocolate is available in 100g, 150g and 360g sizes.

Swiss Milk Chocolate with Honey and Almond Nougat (10%)
Ingredients: Sugar, whole milk powder, cocoa butter, cocoa mass, honey (3%), milk fat, almonds (1.6%), emulsifier (soya lecithin), egg white, flavouring (vanillin).
Milk chocolate contains: Cocoa solids 28% minimum. Milk solids 14% minimum.

TOBLERONE white chocolare

Creamy White Chocolate with Honey and Almond Nougat. This delicious chocolate is available in a 360g size.

Swiss white chocolate with honey and almond nougat (10%)
Ingredients: Sugar, whole milk powder, cocoa butter, honey (3%), almonds (1.6%), emulsifier (soya lecithin), egg white, flavouring (vanillin)

TOBLERONE dark chocolare

Rich Dark chocolate with Honey and Almond Nougat. This delicious chocolate is available in a 360g size.

Swiss plain chocolate with honey and almond nougat (10%)
Ingredients: Cocoa mass, sugar, milk fat, honey (3%), almonds (1.6%), cocoa butter, emulsifier (soya lecithin), egg white. Plain chocolate contains: Cocoa solids 50% minimum.
